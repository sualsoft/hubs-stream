The Challenge

Business applications are primarily data collectors, processors and providers. As such, no business application is an island; each must collect the data they require from various sources and provide it to the same in order to function within the overall business ecosystem.

The sources of interaction with each business application are generally not well known at the time of the application’s creation, and often change during the lifetime of that application in the business. Compounding this is the fact that many of those sources are remote and not within the control of the business itself

Additionally, the business and technology world is constantly evolving and demands on these system are growing to accommodate near-real-time adaptation within this new world – AI, Machine learning, in-flight analytics and decision making, IOT, etc.

How does the business manage the rapidly evolving landscape of applications in its ecosystem and ensure that the whole becomes and remains more than the sum of its parts?
